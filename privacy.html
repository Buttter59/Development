Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The short version

TyperTester does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your typing records are stored only in your own browser's localStorage and are never sent anywhere. We have no servers storing your information, no accounts, and no analytics that identify you personally.

What data we store locally

TyperTester stores the following data in your browser's localStorage — on your device only:

• Your typing test scores (WPM, accuracy, difficulty, date)
• Your theme preference (light or dark mode)

This data never leaves your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's site data or by using the "Clear All Records" button in the app.

Cookies

TyperTester does not use cookies. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or session cookies of any kind.

Third-party services

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces (JetBrains Mono and Syne). This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers to fetch the font files. Google may log this request per their own privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other user tracking analytics service. We do not know who uses our site, how long they stay, where they come from, or any other behavioral data.

Advertising

TyperTester shows no advertisements. We do not work with advertising networks. We do not sell data to advertisers.

Children's privacy

TyperTester is appropriate for all ages. Since we collect no personal data and require no registration, there are no special considerations for children's use.

Your rights

Since we store no personal data on our servers, there is no data to request, modify, or delete from our end. All locally stored data can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last changed. We don't anticipate changes that would reduce your privacy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how TyperTester handles data, reach out through our website's contact page. We'll respond promptly.
